Replaces the entire data directory with the contents of the chosen backup. Your current data is moved aside (kept on disk for emergency rollback). Pica must be restarted after a successful restore.

Automatic backups

Pica checks every 5 minutes whether a scheduled backup is due. Retention is applied after each scheduled backup; manual backups also count toward retention but won't trigger a prune on their own.
